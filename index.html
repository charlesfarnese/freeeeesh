A timely collaboration between some of London's most loved restaurants and their producers - each week local restaurants curate their own box of quality produce from their suppliers, you take your pick, and we deliver it straight to your door.

Think boxes filled with scrumptious vegetables, fruits, meats and fish, cheese, fresh eggs, milk, chantarelles, pickles from the pantry, and plenty more.

At a time when the restaurants have shut up shop, producers are struggling, and supermarket queues are out of the door, there's no better time to stay home and support local.
